Climax

A climax in my life would be when a video of mine got showcased in the KHOU 11 news channel. In my 7th-grade broadcasting class, I was presented with the opportunity to make a video for the class trip to the recording and production studio for the company. This allowed me to showcase my creativity. I was able to record and edit a 35-45-second video of my classmates as well as exhibit the work we did in the broadcasting class. The video was broadcast live to over 50,000 Houstonians, with me being featured in a brief interview. This occurred during both, my 7th and 8th grades in the broadcasting class. This event, which was covered in a KHOU 11 news story, was the pinnacle of my life to date since it brought my love of video production together with an unforgettable opportunity to present my work to a large audience.
