Thematic Statement

"To grow, create, and inspire, using my passion for technology and learning to shape a meaningful future."

The above is a quote I found a while ago that resonates with me. It is what I use to keep me on track and motivated. I feel like this quote represents me well because that is my ultimate goal in life, to help shape a more meaningful future so that the world can continue to evolve and become a better place. Creation is also an aspect of my life, it is what I do whenever I am publishing a new page or working on a school project, I am always showcasing creation and that is one of the aspects I love about this quote.
